## Top Priority Risks
- Ownership roster drift can undermine both governance transparency and downstream fraud-and-abuse reviews because the center bills Medicare for services tied to physician relationships.
- Vendor hospitality requires immediate review because recurring meals and receptions create inducement optics even when no cash changes hands.
- Exclusion-screening gaps should be closed before the next monthly board cycle because ownership and contractor changes can create exposure quickly.

## Planned Response
- Reconcile ownership records across corporate, compliance, and payer-facing systems within 30 days.
- Require preapproval for any vendor event that involves physician attendees, document business purpose, and cap frequency.
- Route every hotline contact into the case management log and report monthly metrics to the compliance committee.
- Expand exclusion-screening attestation to include new owners, contractors, and temporary staff before they begin work.
