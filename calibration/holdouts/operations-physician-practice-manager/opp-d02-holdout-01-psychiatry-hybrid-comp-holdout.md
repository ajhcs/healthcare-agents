## Stark/AKS Compliance Checklist
- FMV opinion must support the stipend, base salary, and incentive rate as separate compensation elements.
- Productivity credit must exclude work performed independently by therapists, APPs, or collaborative care staff unless a compliant billing basis exists.
- The arrangement should remain commercially reasonable based on access need, retention risk, and integrated behavioral health strategy.
- Amendment execution is required before the effective date.
- Counsel review remains necessary for final contract language and any state-specific employment restrictions.
