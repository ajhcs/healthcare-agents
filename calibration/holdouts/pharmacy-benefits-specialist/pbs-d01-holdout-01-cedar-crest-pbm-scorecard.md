## Hidden Cost Assessment
- Spread pricing applies to 16 percent of retail claims.
- MAC updates are weekly with no advance notice for all NDCs.
- Audit rights are limited to summary-level data.
- Specialty exclusions create leakage outside the main contract.

## Overall Assessment
The contract is workable but not optimal. Pricing is acceptable, yet weak transparency and limited audit rights reduce sponsor leverage and should be renegotiated.
