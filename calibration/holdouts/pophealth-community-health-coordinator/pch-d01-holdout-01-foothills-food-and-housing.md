## Community Input Summary
**Public health department input**: Eastbrook County Public Health emphasized food access, substandard housing, and transportation as the issues most likely to worsen avoidable hospital use.

**Medically underserved population input**: Older adults, seasonal farmworkers, and families using the emergency department for primary care described the need for evening clinic access, closer pharmacy options, and utility support.

**Written comments on prior CHNA**: Two comments from the 2023 CHNA requested stronger action on transportation and more coordination with faith-based food pantries. Both themes were incorporated into this cycle.

- Track each priority need through quarterly dashboard review
