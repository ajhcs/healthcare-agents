## Community Input Summary
**Public health department input**: Harbor City Department of Public Health identified maternal morbidity, child asthma, assault injury, and food access as the city's highest-priority preventable conditions.

**Medically underserved population input**: Spanish-speaking mothers, asylum-seeking families, residents of public housing, and youth affected by violence all asked for neighborhood-based services, trusted navigators, and evening access.

**Written comments on prior CHNA**: Eleven written comments on the 2023 CHNA asked for more transparency around progress, stronger neighborhood partnerships, and more data on language access. These comments were summarized for the board and used to sharpen the 2026 implementation strategy.
