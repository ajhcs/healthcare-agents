# Medicare Outreach Event Plan

**Event Name**: Medicare and Your Employer Coverage Transition Workshop
**Event Type**: Medicare 101
**Date/Time**: 2026-08-13, 12:00 PM to 1:15 PM
**Location**: North Quill Manufacturing Training Center, 2400 Alloy Road, Westhaven
**Target Audience**: Employees and spouses within 9 months of age 65
**Expected Attendance**: 28
**Event Lead**: Maren Pike, Benefits Education Coordinator

## Pre-Event Preparation
- Venue reserved with wheelchair-accessible entrance, captioned presentation capability, and nearby employee parking
- Materials prepared:
  - Medicare and You 2026 booklet excerpts on enrollment timing and employer coverage coordination
  - Employer coverage transition handout explaining current employment coverage, retiree coverage, and COBRA distinctions
  - SHIP referral sheet for private counseling outside the workplace
  - Large-print packet for low-vision attendees
  - FAQ sheet on Part B and Part D late enrollment penalties
- Technology:
  - Closed-caption-enabled slide deck
  - Projection screen and room audio check completed 24 hours before session
- Staffing:
  - 1 SHIP counselor for education and questions
  - 1 HR moderator to manage employee benefit questions
  - 1 accessibility support staff member
- Promotion:
  - HR email bulletin sent 3 weeks and 3 days before event
  - Payroll insert in final July cycle
  - Intranet banner on retirement planning page
- Compliance review:
  - Session labeled as educational only
  - No plan enrollment forms or plan-specific comparison handouts onsite
  - Attendance sign-in limited to room count and accommodation needs, not sales follow-up
  - Script includes referral to SHIP for individual counseling

## Event Agenda
| Time | Activity | Lead |
|------|----------|------|
| 12:00 PM | Welcome, purpose, and educational event ground rules | Maren Pike |
| 12:05 PM | Medicare Parts A and B when you are still working | SHIP Counselor |

| 12:35 PM | COBRA, retiree coverage, and common penalty mistakes | SHIP Counselor |

## Post-Event Follow-Up
- Evaluation forms reviewed by benefits office and SHIP partner within 2 business days
- Interested attendees receive SHIP scheduling instructions only if they affirmatively request follow-up
- HR updates retirement resources page with workshop slides and public Medicare references
- Event attendance and topic questions summarized for annual benefits education planning
- Thank-you note sent to SHIP regional office
