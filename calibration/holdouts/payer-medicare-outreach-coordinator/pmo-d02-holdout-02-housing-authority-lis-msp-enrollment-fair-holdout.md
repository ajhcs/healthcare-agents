# Medicare Outreach Event Plan

**Event Name**: Save Money on Medicare Enrollment Day
**Event Type**: Benefits Enrollment
**Date/Time**: 2026-11-12, 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM
**Location**: Willow Crest Housing Commons, 88 Lantern Court, East Briar
**Target Audience**: Low-income Medicare beneficiaries and caregivers in subsidized housing
**Expected Attendance**: 55
**Event Lead**: Rina Calder, Community Benefits Supervisor

## Pre-Event Preparation
- Venue confirmed with private counseling rooms, wheelchair-accessible route, and multilingual welcome desk
- Materials prepared:
  - LIS application guides using current SSA public instructions
  - State MSP application packets and document checklist
  - QMB billing-rights handout referencing federal balance-billing protections
  - Medicare and You 2026 excerpts on getting help with costs
  - Large-print forms and language access request cards
- Technology:
  - Secure laptops for public benefit portals
  - Two privacy screens and one document scanner
  - Locked file tote for paper copies pending handoff
- Staffing:
  - 3 SHIP counselors
  - 2 housing authority navigators
  - 1 interpreter coordinator
- Promotion:
  - Door flyers delivered to all residential buildings
  - Resident council announcement two weeks before event
  - Automated reminder call 72 hours before event
  - Table tents at congregate meal site
- Compliance review:
  - Event limited to affordability education and application support
  - No plan-specific marketing, no insurance carrier materials, no enrollment steering
  - Privacy workflow reviewed for handling Medicare numbers and income records

## Event Agenda
| Time | Activity | Lead |
|------|----------|------|
| 10:00 AM | Welcome, privacy instructions, and event overview | Rina Calder |
| 10:10 AM | Short presentation on LIS, MSP, and QMB protections | Lead SHIP Counselor |
| 10:35 AM | What documents to bring and how application pathways work | Housing Navigator Team |
| 10:50 AM | One-on-one screening and application assistance begins | SHIP Counselors and Navigators |
| 12:30 PM | Midday regroup and interpreter triage check | Interpreter Coordinator |
| 1:45 PM | Final referral instructions for incomplete applications | Rina Calder |

## Post-Event Follow-Up
- All incomplete packets reviewed within 2 business days for missing documents
- Residents receive follow-up calls only if they request application assistance
- Secure document copies transferred per housing authority privacy protocol
- Summary report prepared for housing authority leadership and SHIP regional coordinator
- Partner thank-you note sent to resident council and interpreter agency
