## Outcome
- Outbreak declared over: 2026-03-28

- Total cases: 3 | Total affected units: 1

## Lessons Learned & Recommendations
1. Remove sink-adjacent medication and supply handling from oncology workflows where immunocompromised patients have central lines.
2. Align outbreak response with the facility water management program so remediation, verification, and escalation are documented in one control framework.
3. Maintain rapid environmental assessment capability for drains, aerators, and splash zones when unusual gram-negative clusters emerge.
4. Reinforce oral care and line-access practices that keep sterile or clean supplies away from wet surfaces.
5. Recheck current policy expectations if the report will support live survey readiness beyond the next quarterly regulatory refresh.
