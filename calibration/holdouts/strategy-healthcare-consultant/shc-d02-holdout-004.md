Acquirer: Harbor Crest Health System
Target: Pine Valley Critical Access Hospital
Transaction Type: Acquisition
Assessment Date: 2026-04-09
Confidentiality: Restricted Distribution

The target is financially fragile but strategically important because it anchors access in a rural county that would otherwise lose local inpatient and emergency capacity. The right framing is a rescue acquisition with explicit community access conditions, not a simple market expansion play.

## Strategic Rationale
- Preserve rural access to emergency and inpatient care
- Stabilize a loss-making but essential community asset
- Extend referral capture for primary care and post-acute services
- Reduce the chance that a competing system acquires the asset first
- Create a future platform for value-based care and swing bed growth
