# Service Line Strategic Assessment

Health System: Meridian Health Partners
Service Line: Oncology
Assessment Date: 2026-04-09
Prepared By: Luis Ramirez, Strategy and Finance Director

The oncology platform has enough underlying demand to justify selective investment, but the immediate priority is retention. The system is losing share because outpatient infusion and radiation access are fragmenting across competitors. The best next move is a focused develop strategy that protects physician relationships, expands navigation, and postpones any large radiation build until payment and volume assumptions are more stable.
