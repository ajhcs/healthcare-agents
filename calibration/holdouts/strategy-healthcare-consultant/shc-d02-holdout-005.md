# M&A Due Diligence Summary

Acquirer: Meridian Health Partners
Target: Summit Spine and Pain Associates
Transaction Type: Joint Venture
Assessment Date: 2026-04-09
Confidentiality: Restricted Distribution

The practice is profitable, but the owner group is facing succession pressure and wants a path to monetize some value without giving up all autonomy. A joint venture is the cleanest structure because it captures ambulatory spine volume while limiting concentration risk and keeping the physicians engaged in governance.

## Strategic Rationale
- Secure ambulatory spine volume
- Improve access in the east referral corridor
- Reduce leakage to competing systems
- Give retiring physicians a liquidity path without a full sale
- Build a platform for pain management and physical therapy coordination
