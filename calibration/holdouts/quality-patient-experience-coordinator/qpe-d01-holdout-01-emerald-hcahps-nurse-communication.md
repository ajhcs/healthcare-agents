# Patient Experience Improvement Plan

**Hospital/Unit**: Emerald Orthopedic Pavilion, 4 West  
**HCAHPS Composite**: Communication with Nurses  
**Current Percentile Rank**: 29th | **Target Percentile Rank**: 72nd  
**Measurement Period**: Rolling four quarters ending November 2025  
**Plan Owner**: Denise Morales, MSN, RN

## Current State Analysis
- Top-box rate: 57% (National mean: 67%)
- Percentile rank: 29
- Trend (last 4 quarters): Declining
- Priority Index items: courtesy/respect, explaining medicines in a way patients understand
- Comment theme analysis: inconsistent bedside introductions, rushed medication explanations, delayed updates

## Root Cause Analysis
| Contributing Factor | Evidence | Impact |
|--------------------|----------|--------|
| Inconsistent bedside scripts | Rounding audits showed five distinct introductions | Patients did not hear a standard greeting |
| Medication teaching at discharge only | 61% of patients received teaching after the discharge order was entered | Patients missed teach-back opportunities |
| Slow call-light response | Evening response time averaged 8.7 minutes | Patients described staff as inattentive |
| Limited leader rounding | Nurse manager rounding occurred on 39% of admissions | Small issues became complaints |

## Interventions
| Intervention | Target Behavior | Responsible | Timeline | Expected Impact |
|-------------|----------------|------------|---------|----------------|
| Standard bedside introduction script | Each encounter starts with name, role, and purpose | Unit educator | 30 days | Higher courtesy/respect scores |
| Medication teach-back during administration | Nurses explain purpose and side effects before giving meds | Clinical nurse specialist | 45 days | Higher communication-about-medicines scores |
| Daily escalation huddle for slow response units | Leaders remove barriers before discharge | Nurse manager | Immediate | Lower complaint volume |
| Rounding dashboard for low-performing shifts | Direct attention to late-evening delays | Nursing director | 14 days | Better responsiveness |
