# Reference Style Guide

## Tone

Write as a senior practitioner producing a real deliverable. Do not narrate the process. Do not add hedging or meta-commentary.

## Granularity

Keep each exemplar dense enough to demonstrate competence, but short enough to remain maintainable.

## PHI Safety

All patient and organizational data must be clearly synthetic.

- Use fictional names that are not celebrity names.
- Use synthetic MRNs.
- Use synthetic NPIs.
- Use generic facility names.
- Avoid combinations that could plausibly identify a real person.

## Citations

Only cite public, verifiable sources. Do not invent regulations, codes, or policy references.

## Structure

Follow the deliverable template from the corresponding agent prompt. The exemplar should show the template filled in, not a different format.
