## Findings
- The contract is commercially viable but underpriced against peer benchmarks in every major service category except ancillary.
- The recoupment clause is the clearest financial risk because it permits retroactive offsets with limited notice.
- The deemed acceptance language creates a unilateral amendment pathway that should not survive renewal.
- Medicare Advantage and exchange products are carrying the same administrative terms as PPO, which weakens portfolio segmentation.
- The current fee schedule is strongest on volume, not on margin, so the renewal ask should be anchored to E/M and outpatient facility lift.
