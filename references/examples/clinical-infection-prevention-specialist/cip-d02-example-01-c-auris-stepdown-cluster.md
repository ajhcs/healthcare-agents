## Lessons Learned & Recommendations
1. High-risk transfer screening for Candida auris exposure must occur at admission and at inter-unit transfer so contact precautions start before room placement.
2. Mobile shared equipment requires unit-level ownership, disinfection accountability, and audit visibility during transmission events.
3. Environmental product selection for emerging pathogens should be preapproved in advance; relying on routine disinfectant stock delayed early containment.
4. Agency staff onboarding for enhanced precautions should be completed at shift start during outbreak periods, not through passive policy distribution alone.
5. Continue quarterly point-prevalence drills on high-risk units and review current CDC and public health guidance if policy requirements change.
