## Litigation Hold Protocol
- All destruction activity stops immediately upon written or electronic notice from Legal, Compliance, or Risk Management.
- HIM maintains a litigation hold register with matter name, custodian, affected record classes, notice date, release date, and approving attorney.
- Destruction manifests are screened against the litigation hold register before vendor pickup or media sanitization.
- Release of a hold requires written authorization from Legal Counsel.

## Operational Notes
- If a state or federal requirement changes, the longest applicable period controls until this schedule is formally revised.
- Business associates handling archived PHI must provide documented destruction controls at contract termination.
- Patient requests for access do not alter retention periods; they affect disclosure workflow only.
- Records connected to open audits, repayment reviews, or government investigations are removed from destruction eligibility until written clearance is issued.
