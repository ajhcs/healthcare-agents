  - 2 SHIP-trained counselors
  - 1 library host
  - 1 registration support volunteer
- Promotion:
  - Flyers posted at library branches and senior meal sites

- Compliance review:
  - Flyer labeled educational event
  - No plan-specific logos, no enrollment forms, no required sign-in
