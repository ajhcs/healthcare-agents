# Patient Experience Improvement Plan

**Hospital/Unit**: Emerald Orthopedic Pavilion, 4 West  
**HCAHPS Composite**: Communication with Nurses  
**Current Percentile Rank**: 31st | **Target Percentile Rank**: 70th  
**Measurement Period**: Rolling four quarters ending December 2025  
**Plan Owner**: Denise Morales, MSN, RN

## Current State Analysis
- Top-box rate: 58% (National mean: 67%)
- Percentile rank: 31
- Trend (last 4 quarters): Declining
- Priority Index items: nurse courtesy/respect, explaining medicines in a way patients understand
- Comment theme analysis: rushed answers, poor medication explanations, slow response to call lights

## Root Cause Analysis
| Contributing Factor | Evidence | Impact |
|--------------------|----------|--------|
| Inconsistent bedside scripts | Observed rounding showed six different nurse introduction styles | Patients did not hear a consistent explanation |
| Medication teaching at the end of the shift | Audit found discharge teaching delayed until transport arrived | Patients missed teach-back opportunities |
| Call-light response variability | Median response time was 8.4 minutes on evenings | Patients described the unit as inattentive |
| Limited leader rounding | Nurse manager rounding occurred on 42% of admissions | Service recovery was often too late |

## Interventions
| Intervention | Target Behavior | Responsible | Timeline | Expected Impact |
|-------------|----------------|------------|---------|----------------|
| Standard bedside introduction script and purpose statement | Every nurse opens each encounter the same way | Unit educator | 30 days | Improve courtesy/respect comments |
| Medication teach-back at point of administration | Nurses explain purpose and side effects before the dose | Clinical nurse specialist | 45 days | Raise communication-about-medicines scores |
| Two-hour leader rounding huddles for low-rated shifts | Charge nurse resolves service issues before discharge | Nurse manager | Immediate | Reduce unresolved concerns |
| Daily call-light dashboard review | Identify units with slow response trends | Nursing director | 14 days | Improve responsiveness composite |
