## Community Input Summary
**Public health department input**: Northport County Public Health and South Bay County Health District identified behavioral health access, maternal mortality inequities, and housing instability as the three most urgent cross-sector needs.

**Medically underserved population input**: Youth in recovery, Spanish-speaking parents, residents experiencing homelessness, and older adults living in isolated rural pockets said the most important barriers were long waits, transport gaps, stigma, and fragmented referral pathways.

**Written comments on prior CHNA**: Nine written comments were logged on the 2023 CHNA. The most common themes were the need for after-hours behavioral health care, stronger partnership with domestic violence agencies, and more public reporting on CHNA progress.
