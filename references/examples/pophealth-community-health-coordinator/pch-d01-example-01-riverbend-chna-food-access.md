## Community Input Summary
**Public health department input**: Mason County Health Department and Belmont County Public Health jointly identified food insecurity, preventable diabetes complications, and missed appointments caused by transportation barriers as top cross-sector concerns.

**Medically underserved population input**: Input from residents receiving SNAP, uninsured adults, older adults living alone, and Latino farmworker families emphasized evening clinic access, language-concordant outreach, and help obtaining healthy food near home.

**Written comments on prior CHNA**: Four written comments were received on the 2023 CHNA. Two requested more behavioral health access, one requested additional transportation support, and one asked for better communication in Spanish. All four themes were incorporated into the 2026 prioritization discussion.
