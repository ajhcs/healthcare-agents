## Executive Summary
The largest gains came from orthopedic implant management and restoring committed contract compliance in categories that had drifted through off-contract purchasing. Realized savings exceeded $4.0M without service disruption, and savings methodology was reconciled to actual purchased volume rather than projected conversion volume.
