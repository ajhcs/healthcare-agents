# Value Analysis Product Request

**Request Date**: 2026-02-18
**Requestor**: Mira Solis, MSN, RN, Wound Care Program Manager, Inpatient Nursing
**Product Requested**: SilvraFlex Border Foam 6x6, Lattice Biocare, Catalog SFB-66
**Current Product**: GuardFoam Silicone Border 6x6, Meridian Clinical, Catalog GFS-66

## Clinical Rationale
- The wound team requested SilvraFlex Border Foam for sacral and heel pressure injuries with heavy exudate in patients who fail wear-time goals on the current formulary foam.
- During a 30-day supervised evaluation on two units, nursing documented fewer unscheduled dressing changes for a defined subset of high-output wounds.
- The requested product did not demonstrate superior outcomes for routine postoperative dressing use, and the committee limited the request to wound-care-directed use rather than broad floor stock replacement.
- Supporting references reviewed by the subcommittee included public FDA labeling, manufacturer instructions for use, and internal utilization data from the evaluation period.

## Committee Decision
- Decision: Approved with conditions
- Condition 1: Product restricted to wound care consult service and designated skin-integrity champions.
- Condition 2: Product stocked only in wound carts and not added to general med-surg par locations.
- Condition 3: Six-month review required on utilization, dressing-change frequency, and contract compliance effect.
- Condition 4: Current formulary foam remains first-line product for routine postoperative use and uncomplicated pressure injury prevention.
