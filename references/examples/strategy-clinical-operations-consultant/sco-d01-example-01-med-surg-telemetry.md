The med-surg and telemetry units are covering the census, but the schedule relies too much on premium labor and overtime. The staffing grid needs to protect 42 CFR 482.23 expectations while reducing unnecessary agency coverage.

| Telemetry day | 7 RN | 2 support | Use acuity-based assignments |
| Telemetry night | 6 RN | 2 support | Limit surge coverage to census triggers |

## Flex Plan
| Trigger | Action | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Census above 34 on med-surg | Activate float RN and one extra CNA | Nurse manager |
| Two or more call-outs on a shift | Use internal per-diem before agency | House supervisor |
| Telemetry acuity spike | Reassign charge nurse to direct care for four hours | Clinical administrator |

## Safety & Quality Impact Assessment
- The recommended grid lowers the chance of missed care, pressure injuries, and overtime-driven burnout.
- The plan preserves safety by matching the flex pool to census spikes rather than converting every spike into a permanent FTE increase.
