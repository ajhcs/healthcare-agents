## Laboratory Results
- Urinary antigen testing was positive in five cases.
- One sputum culture produced Legionella pneumophila serogroup 1 with matching WGS.
- The environmental isolate from the water heater matched the clinical cluster.

## Environmental Investigation
- The hot-water return loop temperature was 111 F, below the target range.
- The facility had stagnant water outlets on two floors that had not been flushed since December.
- Maintenance logs showed a missed water management review after a boiler repair.

## Control Measures Implemented
- [x] Hyperchlorinated the hot-water system - February 11, 2026
- [x] Posted shower restrictions for the affected wing - February 12, 2026
- [x] Instituted daily water temperature and flushing logs - February 13, 2026

## Recommendations
1. Adopt a formal water management plan with accountability and signoff.
2. Add contracted lab notification timing to the facility's surveillance agreement.
3. Reassess exposure controls before reopening the affected wing.
