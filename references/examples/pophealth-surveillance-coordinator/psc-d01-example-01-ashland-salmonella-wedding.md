## Laboratory Results
- Stool cultures from six cases matched Salmonella enterica serotype Enteritidis.
- The state laboratory reported a shared PFGE pattern and WGS cluster.
- The caterer submitted a retained-food sample with the same isolate profile.

## Environmental Investigation
- Cold holding temperatures for the salad table were 52-56 F during the reception.
- The caterer could not document time-and-temperature control for the prepared salads.
- The venue had no written log for off-site food transport and storage.

## Control Measures Implemented
- [x] Issued food handling advisory to the caterer and venue - March 20, 2026
- [x] Notified exposed guests and recommended symptom monitoring - March 20, 2026
- [x] Removed the caterer from event service until corrective action review - March 22, 2026

## Recommendations
1. Require time-and-temperature logs for all catered cold foods.
2. Train event venues on immediate public health notification when multiple guests report gastrointestinal illness.
3. Add catered-event reporting to the jurisdiction's routine foodborne surveillance review.
