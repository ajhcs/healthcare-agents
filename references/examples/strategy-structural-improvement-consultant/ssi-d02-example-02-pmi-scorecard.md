# Post-Merger Integration Scorecard

The integration is moving, but the scorecard shows that finance and IT are ahead of culture and operating-model alignment. A merger can hit the synergy line and still miss the real integration target.

Yellow overall with finance and supply chain ahead of schedule, clinical integration in the middle, and culture still needing work.

- The integration team should use fewer messages and more visible local wins.

The scorecard is designed to separate smooth-looking dashboards from actual integration progress, with attention to people, revenue cycle, and compliance before the synergy count is declared real. The key question is not whether the merger is on schedule; it is whether the operating model can actually survive contact with the day-to-day work.
