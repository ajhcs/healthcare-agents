## Stark Law Analysis
Applicable exception: physician recruitment under 42 CFR 411.357(e).

The hospital is recruiting into a documented shortage area, and the agreement includes relocation support, a base income guarantee, and call coverage expectations. The file still needs a clean description of the service area, the physician's relocation commitment, and the repayment provisions if the physician leaves early. The arrangement is low to moderate risk if the documentation is completed before execution.

## Anti-Kickback Statute Analysis
Closest safe harbor: practitioner recruitment under 42 CFR 1001.952(n).

The arrangement should stay within the safe harbor structure by keeping recruitment benefits tied to the relocation need rather than to referral volume. The compliance file should confirm:
- The recruiting area qualifies as a shortage area or otherwise fits the recruitment rationale
- Recruitment support is not conditioned on referral volume
- Call coverage and clinic schedule obligations are documented in writing
- The physician's practice commitments are stated clearly enough to monitor compliance
