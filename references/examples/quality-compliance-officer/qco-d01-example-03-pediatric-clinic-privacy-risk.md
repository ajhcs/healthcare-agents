# Annual Compliance Risk Assessment

**Organization**: Harborlight Pediatrics  
**Assessment Period**: Fiscal Year 2026  
**Compliance Officer**: Jordan Lee, CHC  
**Date Completed**: 2026-04-09  
**Approved By**: Compliance Committee on 2026-04-09

## Methodology
This assessment reviewed portal access settings, marketing approvals, consent logs, privacy complaints, and workforce training records. The review emphasized HIPAA privacy and security at 45 CFR Parts 160 and 164, along with the OIG General Compliance Program Guidance issued in November 2023. The goal was to identify risks that could be corrected within one fiscal year.

## Risk Universe
| # | Risk Area | Description | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Score | Existing Controls | Residual Risk | Priority |

| 1 | Portal access settings | Parent proxy access is not consistently reviewed when custody or guardianship changes occur. | 4 | 4 | 16 | Front-desk verification | Medium | High |
| 2 | Marketing consent | Digital ads and campaign emails are sent before the approval record is fully documented. | 3 | 4 | 12 | Marketing review queue | Medium | Medium |
| 3 | Minimum necessary access | Staff can open broad chart views when they only need appointment or referral data. | 4 | 4 | 16 | Role-based access design | Medium | High |
| 4 | Complaint closure | Privacy complaints are closed by the supervisor without a formal remediation note. | 3 | 3 | 9 | Manager follow-up | Low | Medium |

## Top Priority Risks
- Proxy access controls must be tightened because pediatric privacy issues often arise when family status changes faster than system permissions.
- Broad chart access creates avoidable exposure because the minimum necessary standard requires role-specific access discipline.
- Marketing consent needs a single approval record so the clinic can prove campaign timing and authorization.

## Planned Response
- Add a monthly proxy-access reconciliation tied to guardianship and custody changes.
- Narrow chart permissions for registration and scheduling staff.
- Require a written marketing approval log before any campaign launch.
- Standardize privacy complaint closure notes and trend them at the compliance committee.
