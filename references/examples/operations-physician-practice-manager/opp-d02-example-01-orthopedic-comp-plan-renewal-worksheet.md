## Design Rationale
- Current production trend projects 9,480 wRVUs for FY2026, positioning the surgeon modestly above the specialty median.
- Base salary below target total cash creates meaningful upside without placing the provider below local recruitment market expectations.
- Incentive rate was held inside the valuation opinion support band to avoid drift above supportable $/wRVU.
- Separate trauma call compensation is justified by documented uncompensated coverage frequency and should remain outside the productivity rate.
- Medical director duties require contemporaneous time logs and an annual duty inventory to maintain FMV support.
