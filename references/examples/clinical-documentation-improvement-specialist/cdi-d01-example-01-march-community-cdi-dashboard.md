## Leadership Interpretation
CDI review coverage remained above target for the third consecutive month, with ICU and stepdown census fully covered and med-surg review coverage improved through CAC-based prioritization. Query response rate improved after escalation routing was tightened to a 48-hour follow-up standard.

The largest opportunity remained malnutrition documentation, where dietitian-supported findings were present but attending confirmation lagged. Heart failure and AKI specificity work continued to produce the strongest physician agreement and the most reliable working-to-final DRG alignment.

PSI-sensitive documentation remained manageable, with March queries concentrated in postoperative respiratory failure, pressure injury POA clarification, and perioperative sepsis exclusions. No internally audited queries referenced payment, CC/MCC status, or quality scores.
